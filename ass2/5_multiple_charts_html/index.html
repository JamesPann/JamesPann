Marriages and Divorces

Marriage Status

Marriage rates have fallen in most countries. Australia decreases from 6.6 to 4.6, while Hungary falls from 5.9 to 3.7 between 1990 to 2013, then rises back to 5.2 in 2017.

The average age at marriage rose from 23.9 to 27.4. This may because women are more inclined to pursue what they like when they are young.

Same-sex Marriage

From the chart, more and more countries recognize homosexuality. In 2019, Homosexuality is legal in 133 countries, which means people are more and more open-minded and respect the sexual orientation of different individuals.

Americas and Western European countries have a higher degree of recognition of homosexuality. Besides, Chile and Italy even granted rights to homosexual people. However, most Asian and African countries do not recognize homosexuality in the current law.
